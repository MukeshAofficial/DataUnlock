Frequently Asked Questions

You just need to enter a URL and it creates a chatbot instantly?

Yes, for our 'Enter URL & Get Chatbot' feature, simply providing a company's website URL is enough to generate a functional chatbot. For 'Embeddable Chatbot' you can also upload your knowledge base for more specific data.

What kind of company insights can I extract?

You can extract a wide range of insights, including company descriptions, product details, service offerings, contact information, and more, depending on the data available on the company website.

Is the chatbot customizable?

The chatbots are designed to be functional out-of-the-box. Customization options may vary depending on the specific plan. Contact us to discuss advanced customization.

What if I don't have a company URL, but have a document of knowledge base ?

No worries, you can upload your knowledge base document directly in the 'Embeddable Chatbot' feature to create your chatbot.
